## Basic Tips

- Load clothes loosely and don't overfill — let items move freely for a good
  wash and balanced spin.
- Use **HE (high-efficiency) detergent** and don't over-pour; too much soap
  causes residue and extra rinses.
- Leave the lid **open** between uses so the tub can dry out and stay fresh.

## Troubleshooting

- **Won't start** — make sure the lid is closed, the unit is plugged in, and the
  breaker isn't tripped.
- **Won't fill or fills slowly** — confirm both **hot and cold water valves** are
  on and the inlet hoses aren't kinked (these get shut off when [closing the
  cabin](/sop/visits/closing)).
- **Loud / walks during spin** — the load is unbalanced; pause, redistribute the
  clothes, and make sure the washer is level.
- **Won't drain or spin** — redistribute the load and check the drain hose isn't
  kinked.
- **Leaks** — check the fill-hose connections at the back.

## Leaving the Cabin

<Callout type="warning" title="Shut off the washer water">
  When closing up, turn off the water to the washing machine (hot and cold) and
