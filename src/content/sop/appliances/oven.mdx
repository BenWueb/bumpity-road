## Lighting a Surface Burner

<Steps>
  <Step title="Turn to LITE">
    Push the burner knob in and turn it to **LITE**. You'll hear a clicking spark
    — all burners spark until one lights.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust the flame">
    Once the burner lights, turn the knob to set the flame size. The flame should
    match the cookware and **never extend past its sides**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

- The **smallest** burner is the simmer burner (turn to **LO** for delicate,
  low-heat cooking). The **extra-large** burner / Power Boil quickly brings large
  amounts of liquid to a boil.
- Only run a burner with cookware on the grate.

<Callout type="info" title="In a power failure">
  The oven won't work without power, but the **surface burners can be lit with a
  match** — hold a lit match near the burner, then slowly turn the knob to LITE.
</Callout>

## Oven & Self-Clean

- Before running the self-clean cycle, wipe out loose grease and food so it
  doesn't smoke or ignite.
- **Never** line the oven bottom or racks with foil, use commercial oven
  cleaners, or clean the door gasket.

<Callout type="warning" title="Safety">
  Never use the range to heat the room (carbon monoxide risk). The anti-tip
  bracket at the back must stay engaged — re-seat it if the range is ever moved.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

- **Surface burner won't light** — make sure the range is plugged into a live
  outlet and the breaker isn't tripped, the gas is turned on, and the burner caps
  are seated correctly. Clogged burner slots or food on the electrode can also
  stop ignition (clean them).
- **Burners light unevenly or flames are large/yellow** — reseat the burner caps
  and clean clogged slots.
- **Surface burners work but the oven doesn't heat** — the oven gas shut-off lever
  on the regulator (near the gas line) may be off; flip it back on.
- **Oven or display is dead** — check for a blown fuse / tripped breaker, make
  sure it isn't in **Sabbath Mode**, and that **LOC ON** (control lock) isn't
  showing.
- **Door won't open / LOCKED light on** — the oven is still too hot from
  self-clean; press **Cancel/Off** and let it cool.
- **"F" + a number/letter in the display** — function error: press **Cancel/Off**
  and let it cool ~1 hour. If it repeats, cut power for 30 seconds; if it still
  repeats, call for service.
- **Lots of smoke during self-clean** — too much grease; cancel, wipe it out, and
  restart the cycle.
- **Clock flashing** — power outage; reset the clock and any cooking program.
