## Washer

### Loading

1. Sort laundry by color (lights and darks)
2. Do not overload — fill to about 3/4 capacity
3. Add HE (high efficiency) detergent to the dispenser drawer

<Callout type="info" title="Detergent">
  Only use HE detergent. Regular detergent creates too many suds and can damage the machine. The detergent is in the laundry closet above the machines.
</Callout>

### Cycle Settings

- **Normal**: everyday clothes
- **Delicates**: lightweight or fragile fabrics
- **Heavy Duty**: towels, jeans, heavily soiled items
- **Quick Wash**: lightly worn items, 30-minute cycle

### After Washing

Always remove clothes promptly to prevent mildew. Leave the washer door cracked open between loads to let the drum dry.

## Dryer

### Operation

1. Clean the lint trap before every load
2. Load wet clothes loosely — don't pack tightly
3. Select appropriate heat setting:
   - **High**: towels and sheets
   - **Medium**: everyday clothes
   - **Low**: delicates and synthetics
4. Press **Start**

<Callout type="danger" title="Lint Trap">
  Always clean the lint trap before each load. A clogged lint trap is a fire hazard and reduces drying efficiency.
</Callout>

### Dryer Sheets

Dryer sheets are in the cabinet above the machines. Use one per load.
