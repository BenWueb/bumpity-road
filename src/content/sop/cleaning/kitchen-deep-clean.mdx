## Overview

Perform a full kitchen deep clean before leaving the cabin. This ensures the kitchen is ready for the next visitors.

## Cleaning Checklist

<Checklist>
  <ChecklistItem>Wash, dry, and put away all dishes</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Run dishwasher cleaning cycle</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Wipe down all countertops</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Clean stovetop and oven exterior</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Wipe microwave interior and exterior</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Clean sink and faucet</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Empty and wipe refrigerator shelves</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Take out all trash and recycling</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Sweep and mop the floor</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Replace dish towels with fresh ones</ChecklistItem>
</Checklist>

## Counter and Surface Cleaning

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clear all surfaces">
    Remove everything from countertops. Put away food, appliances, and utensils.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Spray and wipe">
    Use the all-purpose cleaner (under the sink) to spray all countertops. Wipe with a clean cloth in circular motions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Backsplash">
    Wipe the backsplash behind the sink and stove. Grease tends to splatter here.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Dry surfaces">
    Dry all surfaces with a clean towel to prevent water spots.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Refrigerator

1. Remove all food you brought — do not leave perishables
2. Check expiration dates on any existing condiments
3. Wipe shelves and drawers with a damp cloth
4. Leave the refrigerator **on** and set to normal temperature

<Callout type="info">
  Communal condiments (ketchup, mustard, etc.) can stay. Discard anything expired or nearly empty.
</Callout>

## Stovetop

1. Remove grates and soak in hot soapy water
2. Wipe the stovetop surface with degreaser spray
3. Scrub stubborn spots with a non-scratch sponge
4. Rinse and dry grates, replace on stovetop

## Supplies Location

All cleaning supplies are stored under the kitchen sink:
- All-purpose cleaner
- Degreaser spray
- Dish soap
- Sponges and scrub brushes
- Paper towels
- Trash bags
