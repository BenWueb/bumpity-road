## Departure Cleaning

Complete the following before leaving:

<Checklist>
  <ChecklistItem>Scrub toilet bowl with brush</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Wipe toilet exterior and base</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Clean sink and faucet</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Wipe mirror</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Clean shower/tub surfaces</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Hang used towels on hooks to dry</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Empty bathroom trash</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Sweep or mop the floor</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Check and refill toilet paper if low</ChecklistItem>
</Checklist>

## Shower / Tub

<Steps>
  <Step title="Spray surfaces">
    Use bathroom cleaner on the shower walls, tub, and fixtures. Let it sit for 2–3 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scrub">
    Use the scrub brush to clean grout lines and any soap scum buildup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rinse">
    Rinse thoroughly with the showerhead. Make sure no cleaner residue remains.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Squeegee">
    If there's a squeegee, use it on the glass door or walls to prevent water spots.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Mirror and Sink

1. Spray glass cleaner on the mirror
2. Wipe with a lint-free cloth in an S-pattern (top to bottom)
3. Spray the sink basin and faucet with all-purpose cleaner
4. Scrub and rinse, then dry with a towel

## Supplies

Bathroom cleaning supplies are in the linen closet in the hallway:
- Bathroom cleaner spray
- Glass cleaner
- Toilet bowl cleaner
- Scrub brush
- Extra toilet paper
- Fresh towels
