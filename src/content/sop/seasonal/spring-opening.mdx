## Overview

Opening the cabin for the season reverses the winterization process. Take your time with the water system — rushing can cause leaks.

## Arrival Inspection

Before turning anything on, walk through the cabin and check for:

<Checklist>
  <ChecklistItem>Signs of water damage (stains on ceilings or walls)</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Critter activity (droppings, nesting material, chewed wires)</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Musty smells (could indicate mold)</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Broken windows or door seals</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Any tripped breakers in the electrical panel</ChecklistItem>
</Checklist>

<Callout type="warning">
  If you find significant water damage or animal intrusion, take photos and contact the property manager before proceeding.
</Callout>

## Water System Startup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Close all faucets">
    Walk through the cabin and close every faucet that was left open for winterization.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the main water valve">
    Turn the main shut-off valve open (handle parallel to pipe).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Turn on the well pump">
    Flip the "Well Pump" breaker to ON. Listen for the pump to engage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check for leaks">
    Slowly walk through the cabin while the system pressurizes. Check under every sink, around toilets, and at the water heater for leaks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Flush the lines">
    Open each faucet one at a time and let it run for 1–2 minutes to flush the antifreeze. Start with the faucet closest to the water supply.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Flush toilets">
    Flush each toilet 2–3 times until the water runs clear.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Turn on the water heater">
    Open the supply valve to the water heater, then switch it to ON. Wait 30–45 minutes for hot water.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Interior Setup

<Checklist>
  <ChecklistItem>Open windows briefly to air out the cabin</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Set thermostat to comfortable temperature</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Check smoke detectors and CO detectors (replace batteries)</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Plug in appliances</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Make beds with fresh linens</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Stock bathroom with toilet paper and towels</ChecklistItem>
</Checklist>

## Exterior Checks

<Checklist>
  <ChecklistItem>Reconnect garden hoses and check for leaks</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Set up patio furniture</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Check the dock and waterfront area</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Look for any winter storm damage (fallen branches, etc.)</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Clear any debris from walkways</ChecklistItem>
</Checklist>
