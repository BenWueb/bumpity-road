## Overview

Winterizing prevents frozen pipes, critter damage, and other cold-weather problems. Complete all steps in order before the last departure of the season (typically late October / early November).

<Callout type="danger" title="Critical">
  Failure to properly winterize can result in burst pipes and significant water damage. Follow every step carefully.
</Callout>

## Water System Shutdown

<Steps>
  <Step title="Turn off the well pump">
    Locate the well pump breaker in the electrical panel (labeled "Well Pump"). Flip it to OFF.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Shut off the main water valve">
    Close the main shut-off valve in the utility closet. Turn the handle perpendicular to the pipe.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open all faucets">
    Open every faucet in the cabin (hot and cold) to drain the lines. Include the outdoor spigot.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Flush toilets">
    Flush each toilet to empty the tank and bowl. Sponge out any remaining water.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add antifreeze to drains">
    Pour 1 cup of RV antifreeze (non-toxic, pink) into each drain: sinks, shower, tub, toilet bowl. This prevents the P-traps from freezing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Turn off the water heater">
    Switch the water heater to OFF. Close the water supply valve to the heater.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Heating

1. Set the thermostat to **55°F (13°C)** — this keeps the cabin above freezing without wasting energy
2. Make sure all heating vents are open and unblocked
3. Check that the furnace filter is clean

<Callout type="warning">
  Never turn the heat completely off in winter. Keeping a minimum temperature prevents condensation, mold, and frozen interior pipes.
</Callout>

## Interior Preparation

<Checklist>
  <ChecklistItem>Remove all perishable food</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Clean out the refrigerator (leave it on at low setting)</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Take out all trash and recycling</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Wash, dry, and store all linens</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Close and lock all windows</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Close all interior doors (to allow heat circulation)</ChecklistItem>

## Exterior

<Checklist>
  <ChecklistItem>Disconnect and store garden hoses</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Bring in patio furniture or secure with covers</ChecklistItem>

  <ChecklistItem>Check that the outdoor spigot is shut off and drained</ChecklistItem>

## Final Walkthrough

Before locking up, do a final lap through the cabin:

1. All water is drained and antifreeze is in traps
2. Thermostat set to 55°F
3. All windows and doors locked
4. No food left out
5. Lights are off (except any on timers)
6. Lock the deadbolt and check it
