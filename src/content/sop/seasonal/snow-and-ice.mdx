## Roof Raking & Ice Dams

<Steps>
  <Step title="Rake the roof">
    Using the roof rake, remove snow from the roof to avoid ice dams. You only
    need to go about **4 ft up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear around the chimney">
    Remove as much snow as possible around the chimney.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="danger" title="Watch the wires near the chimney">
  When removing snow in the chimney area, be extra careful not to damage the
  wires. Use the **push broom** instead of the roof rake for this area.
</Callout>

## Roof Heater

Check the roof heater (heat cable) to make sure it's plugged in when ice dams have
formed.

## Other Snow Care

- Shovel snow off the deck.

<Callout type="warning" title="Don't leave the hall light on">
  The hall light shouldn't be left on for long periods, especially when there's
  snow on the roof. The heat from the light creates an ice dam in the middle of
  the roof.
</Callout>
