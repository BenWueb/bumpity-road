## Wall Thermostat — Controls

### LCD Display

- **ROOM TEMP** — the current room temperature.
- **Flame icon** — appears when the gas fireplace flame is on; it disappears when
  the flame is off (not burning).
- **SET** — appears when the unit is in thermostat mode.

### Keys

<Steps>
  <Step title="ON / UP key">
    Pressing **ON** turns the flame on. If the SET thermostat is on, the flame
    turns off once it reaches the set temperature. For a continuous flame, disable
    thermostat mode first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="OFF / DOWN key">
    Pressing **OFF** turns the gas fireplace flame off, and also turns off the SET
    thermostat mode if it's on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="SET button — set the thermostat">
    Press **SET** — the temperature digits will flash. Press **ON** to raise or
    **OFF** to lower the set temperature. Press **SET** again and the word SET
    appears on the display.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Disabling Thermostat Mode

To disable thermostat mode (for summer, or when you want a constant flame), press
the **OFF** button, or press and hold **SET** until **2 dashes** appear on the
screen.

<Callout type="danger" title="Flame won't auto-off">
  When thermostat mode is disabled, the flame will **not** turn off
  automatically — you'll need to manually push the OFF button.
</Callout>

## Manual Operation (Dead Batteries)

<Callout type="warning">
  If the batteries in the remote receiver or wall thermostat are dead, you'll need
  to turn the gas fireplace on manually.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the pilot light">
    Check whether the pilot light is on. If it is, skip to the last step.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set valve to PILOT">
    Turn the knob on the gas control (valve control) to **PILOT**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Light the pilot">
    Push the control knob all the way in and hold it. Immediately press the piezo
    igniter until the pilot lights.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set valve to ON">
    Turn the gas control to the **ON** position.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Turn the flame on">
    With the pilot lit, press the ON/OFF switch to **ON** to turn the flame on.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Flame & Fan Controls

- **Flame control** — High and Low.
- **Fan speed** — High and Low.

<Callout type="danger" title="Keep things clear">
  The unit can burn items if they're too close — similar to the heated baseboards
  around the cabin. Keep furniture and objects away from the gas fireplace and the
  electric heat baseboards.
</Callout>
