## Cleaning the Cabin

<Checklist>
  <ChecklistItem>All dirty laundry washed</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Strip all beds — leave nothing but the mattress</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Store sheets in dressers, closets, or plastic bins once cleaned</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Fold all blankets into plastic bags or bins</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Place pillows in plastic bags on beds or hang in closets</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Put comforters and covers in plastic bags and leave on beds</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Clean the kitchen</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Sweep and clean all floors — no crumbs to attract mice</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Vacuum all rugs if needed; roll up and sweep underneath too</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Sweep the porch floor</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Take all garbage and recycling home</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Clean the fridge and toss out-of-date items</ChecklistItem>
</Checklist>

<Callout type="warning" title="No chocolate, and no food left to spoil">
  Make sure there's **no chocolate** left in the cabin (it can be stored in the
  refrigerator). Don't leave any food that will expire before someone else comes
  up.
</Callout>

## Before Leaving

<Checklist>
  <ChecklistItem>Turn the water off downstairs and drain the pipes (see Winterizing)</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Leave the heat at 45-50°F</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Make sure all windows are locked</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Leave the doors under the kitchen sink open to keep pipes warm</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Hall lights are OFF</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Ceiling fan switch is OFF</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Check all base heaters for items too close — bedrooms, living areas, porch</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Check the downstairs wash room base heater too (under shelves)</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Turn off water to the washing machine (hot and cold) and drain the hoses</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Close all interior doors</ChecklistItem>
</Checklist>

<Callout type="info" title="Keep doors shut for heat (winter)">
  Keep all bedroom doors shut if not being used. Also keep the downstairs
  bathroom door, the laundry room door, and the door to the basement closed —
  this helps with heating.
</Callout>

<Callout type="danger" title="If you break it, tell someone">
  If something is broken or needs attention, please make a note and call
  [**Jenny, Scott, or Teri**](/sop/reference/contacts#family).
</Callout>

Thank you, and see you next time!
