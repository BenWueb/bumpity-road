Something broken? Got an idea? Use the **Send Feedback** button in the menu. It goes straight to the folks who can fix things.

## How to send feedback

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the menu">
    On a phone, tap the green button in the bottom-right. On a computer, the menu is on the left.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Send Feedback">
    It's near the bottom of the menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write your note">
    A sentence or two is plenty. Clear notes get acted on faster.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    You'll see a quick confirmation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## If you're reporting a bug, include

<Checklist>
  <ChecklistItem>Which page (a link helps)</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>What you were trying to do</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>What happened instead</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Does it happen every time or just once?</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>Your device and browser (iPhone Safari, laptop Chrome, etc.)</ChecklistItem>
</Checklist>

<Callout type="info" title="Ideas are welcome too">
  The same form works for suggestions. Just say whether it's a bug or a feature idea.
</Callout>
