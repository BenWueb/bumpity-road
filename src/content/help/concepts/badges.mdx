Badges are small rewards you earn for using the site. Sign the guestbook? That's a badge. Post 5 blog entries? Another badge. They show up on your [account page](/account).

## How badges work

<Steps>
  <Step title="Use the site">
    Just do the things you'd normally do — post photos, log sightings, sign the guestbook.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch for a pop-up">
    When you earn one, a little toast appears in the corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="See all of yours">
    Open your [account page](/account) to see every badge you've earned, plus ones that are still locked.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Need an account">
  Only signed-in users can earn badges.
</Callout>
