## What you can do here

- Sign the [guestbook](/guestbook) when you visit
- Share photos in the [gallery](/gallery)
- Write stories on the [blog](/blog)
- Track hikes and swimming spots in [adventures](/adventures)
- Log loon sightings on the [loon page](/loon)
- Keep a list of cabin to-dos in [tasks](/todos)
- Earn fun [badges](/help/concepts/badges) as you use the site

Some things you can do without signing in. Others need a quick account. Each feature page will tell you which.

## Three easy first steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the menu">
    On a computer, the menu is on the left. On your phone, tap the green button
    in the bottom-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign the guestbook">
    No account needed. Say hi, pick a color, and submit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Look around">
    Every feature in the menu has its own page. Tap anything that looks
    interesting.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Stuck?">
  The Help menu always has answers. Use the search box on the left side of this
  page to find a topic fast.
</Callout>
