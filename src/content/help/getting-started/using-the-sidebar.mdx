The menu is how you move between pages. On a computer it lives on the left. On a phone it slides in from the side.

## Opening the menu

<Steps>
  <Step title="On a computer">
    The menu is always visible. Click the menu icon at the top to make it smaller or bigger.
  </Step>
  <Step title="On a phone">
    Tap the green button in the bottom-right corner. The menu pops out. It closes by itself when you pick a page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Little labels on menu items

Some items have a small colored tag:

- **NEW** (green) — recently added
- **BUILDING** (amber) — still being worked on, things may change
- **SOON** (blue) — coming, not ready yet

## Sending feedback

There's a **Send Feedback** button near the bottom of the menu. Use it for bug reports or ideas. See [Feedback & Bugs](/help/concepts/feedback-and-bugs) for what to include.

<Callout type="info" title="Help is always in the menu">
  Tap **Help** anywhere in the menu to come back to these pages.
</Callout>
