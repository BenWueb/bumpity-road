Adventures is a list of places worth visiting near the cabin — hikes, swimming holes, towns, viewpoints. Each one is pinned on a map.

<LinkCard
  href="/adventures"
  title="Open adventures"
  description="Browse the list or explore on the map"
/>

## How to use it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse">
    Every adventure has a photo, a description, and tags for season and type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="See it on the map">
    Each card has a map preview. Tap **Open in Google Maps** for directions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your own">
    Tap **New Adventure**. Start typing an address and pick it from the list. Add a photo, pick a season, and save.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter">
    Use the filter buttons to narrow by season or type.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Sign in required">
  Adding or editing adventures needs an [account](/help/getting-started/accounts-and-login).
</Callout>
