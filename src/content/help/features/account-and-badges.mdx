Your account page is your personal hub on Bumpity Road. **Everything you've ever added to the site is logged here**, so you never have to go hunting through different pages to find your own stuff.

<LinkCard
  href="/account"
  title="Open your account"
  description="See everything you've added, all in one spot"
/>

<Callout type="info" title="One stop for your stuff">
  Photo you uploaded last summer? Loon you logged in 2024? Adventure you added two years ago? It's all on this one page.
</Callout>

## What you'll find there

### Your profile

Your name, email, and profile picture — sit at the top. Tap the pencil icon to change them.

### Badges

Every [badge](/help/concepts/badges) you've earned, plus a peek at the ones that are still locked. Earn more by using more parts of the site.

### Everything you've added

Your account page keeps a complete record of your contributions, organized by feature:

<Checklist>
  <ChecklistItem>**Photos** — every picture you've uploaded to the [gallery](/gallery), newest first</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>**Blog posts** — all your [blog](/blog) entries, with thumbnails and comment counts</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>**Adventures** — every spot you've added to the [adventures](/adventures) map</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>**Loon sightings** — your full log of [loon observations](/loon)</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>**Fishing reports** — every fishing trip you've recorded</ChecklistItem>
  <ChecklistItem>**Tasks** — to-dos you created or were assigned, on the [tasks board](/todos)</ChecklistItem>
</Checklist>

Each section is a list you can scroll through. Tap any item to jump straight to its full page on the site.

## Why it's useful

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find old entries fast">
    Looking for a photo you took last 4th of July? Or that hike you logged a while back? Open your account page and scroll the right section — no need to dig through the gallery or adventures pages.
  </Step>
  <Step title="See your contribution at a glance">
    Counts at the top of each section show how many photos, posts, sightings, etc. you've added.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check on your tasks">
    See what's still on your plate, what you've finished, and what others have assigned to you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit or delete">
    From your account page you can hop into anything you've added and edit or remove it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Privacy">
  Your account page only shows **your** entries. Other people see their own when they open theirs.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info" title="Sign in required">
  The account page only exists once you [sign in](/help/getting-started/accounts-and-login).
</Callout>
