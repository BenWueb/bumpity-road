## How to log a sighting

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap add">
    Pick how many loons you saw and what they were doing (swimming, diving, calling).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pin the spot on the map">
    Tap the map where you saw them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use my location">
    On a phone, tap **Use my location** and the pin drops where you are — handy when you're out on the lake.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add weather and notes">
    Pick the current weather and wind. Add any notes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Your sighting joins the list and shows up on the map.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Seeing past sightings">
  Switch between cards, a table, and the map to see sightings different ways.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info" title="Sign in required">
  Logging sightings needs an [account](/help/getting-started/accounts-and-login).
</Callout>
