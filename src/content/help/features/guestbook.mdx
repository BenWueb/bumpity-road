## How to leave a note

<Steps>
  <Step title="Fill in the form">
    Type your name, your note, and pick a color for your card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Your note appears right away. It also shows up on the home page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Come back to edit">
    If you come back on the same phone or computer, you'll see edit and delete buttons on notes you wrote.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Editing later">
  Your notes stay yours as long as you come back from the same browser. If you clear your browser or switch devices, you won't be able to edit them anymore. See [Editing Your Own Entries](/help/concepts/token-ownership).
</Callout>
