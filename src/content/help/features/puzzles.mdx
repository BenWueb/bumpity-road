## How to add one

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap add">
    Fill in the puzzle name and who put it together.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a photo (optional)">
    Upload a picture of the finished puzzle.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    It shows up in the list right away.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Editing later">
  Like the guestbook, puzzles you add stay editable only from the same browser. See [Editing Your Own Entries](/help/concepts/token-ownership).
</Callout>
