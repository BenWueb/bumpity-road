## Adding a puzzle

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap Add a Puzzle">
    Upload a photo of the puzzle so far.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a status">
    Choose **Not finished** if you're leaving it open for others to work on, or **Finished** if it's already complete.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Your name is added as the first contributor and the puzzle shows up right away.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Joining or finishing someone else's puzzle

Open puzzles show an **In progress** badge. Any signed-in user can:

- Tap **I'm working on this** to add yourself as a contributor.
- Tap **Mark Complete** when the last piece goes in — that adds you as a contributor (if you weren't already) and locks the puzzle.

Once a puzzle is marked complete it can no longer be worked on, and every contributor's name is shown in the "completed by" list with the time they joined in.

<Callout type="info" title="Editing">
  The person who started the puzzle can update its photo notes and color. Anyone signed in can join an open puzzle.
</Callout>
