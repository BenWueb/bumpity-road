The blog is for longer stories — a trip report, a memory, a project you finished. Anyone signed in can post.

<LinkCard
  href="/blog"

/>

## How to use it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Read a post">
    The blog page lists every post. Tap one to read the full story.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write a post">
    Tap **Add Post**. Give it a title, write your story, and add a main photo if you like.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit your posts">
    You can edit or delete anything you wrote.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Sign in required">
  Reading and writing both need an [account](/help/getting-started/accounts-and-login).
</Callout>
