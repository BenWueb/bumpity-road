Expenses is the admin-only tracker for everything the cabin spends money on — and everything it might spend money on next. It keeps a running history of costs (with receipt photos), tracks what's been paid, and gives the admins a shared wishlist they can vote on and discuss.

## Incurred vs Wishlist

The page has two tabs, switched at the top:

- **Incurred** — money that's already been spent. Items are grouped by year and month with running totals, and they power the charts. Each one can carry a receipt photo and a paid/unpaid status.
- **Wishlist** — things you're considering buying or doing. These are sorted by vote score so the most-wanted ideas rise to the top, and each can be discussed in comments.

The header shows the **total spent** on the Incurred tab and the **wishlist estimate** on the Wishlist tab.

## Adding an expense

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the form">
    Tap **Add Expense**. The form starts on the tab you're viewing (Incurred or Wishlist), but you can flip the **Incurred / Wishlist** toggle at the top of the form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the basics">
    Add a **title** and **cost**. For incurred items the cost is what you actually paid; for wishlist items it's an **estimated cost**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a category">
    Choose a **category** and, optionally, a **subcategory** for a more detailed breakdown in the charts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the date">
    Incurred items need a **date completed**; wishlist items can have an optional **target date**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add payment details (incurred only)">
    Mark it **Paid** or **Unpaid**, and add a **check number** if you paid by check.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attach a receipt (optional)">
    Upload a **receipt photo** so there's a record of the purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    The item appears under the matching tab right away, and the page switches to that tab.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Receipt photos

Incurred expenses can have a photo of the receipt attached.

- **Upload** a receipt from the form when adding or editing an expense.
- **View** the receipt later by opening the expense — tap the photo to see it full size.
- **Replace or remove** a receipt by editing the expense and uploading a new image or clearing the existing one.

<Callout type="info" title="Keep receipts with the expense">
  Attaching the receipt photo when you log the expense means you'll never have to dig for it later — it travels with the record.
</Callout>

Receipts are only available on **Incurred** items. Switching an item to the Wishlist clears its receipt.

## Marking paid and unpaid

Every incurred expense is either **Paid** or **Unpaid**.

- Set the status when adding or editing the expense.
- Add a **check number** for anything paid by check, so it's easy to reconcile.
- Use the **Payment Status** filter (All / Paid / Unpaid) to quickly find anything still outstanding.

## Categories, subcategories, and filters

Open the **Filters** panel to narrow the list:

- **Category** — show a single category at a time.
- **Payment status** — All, Paid, or Unpaid (Incurred tab).
- **Date range** — from / to dates.
- **Amount range** — minimum / maximum cost.

A badge on the Filters button shows how many filters are active, and **Clear all** resets them at once. Subcategories give you a finer breakdown that shows up when you drill into a category in the charts.

## Charts and totals

On the **Incurred** tab (desktop) you get two visualizations:

- **Bar chart** — spending by category. Click a bar to **drill into its subcategories**; use **All Categories** to go back.
- **Donut** — the overall breakdown with a center total and a percentage legend.

The charts respect your active filters, so you can focus the totals on a date range, category, or amount band.

## Voting and comments (Wishlist)

The Wishlist is collaborative:

- **Vote** items up or down. The list is ordered by score, so the group can see which ideas have the most support.
- **Comment** on an item to discuss details, share links, or note decisions.

## Cards and Details views

Switch between two layouts with the **Cards / Details** toggle:

- **Cards** — a visual grid, grouped by year and month on the Incurred tab.
- **Details** — a flat table that's handy for scanning, editing quickly, or reviewing many items at once.

## Editing and deleting

Open any expense to **edit** its details (including its receipt and paid status) or **delete** it. Deleting asks for confirmation first so nothing disappears by accident.

## Exporting

Use **Export** to download the current tab as an Excel spreadsheet:

- The **Incurred** export includes title, category, date, cost, description, and who added it.
- The **Wishlist** export also includes the vote score and comment count.

<Callout type="success" title="Good habits">
  Log expenses as they happen, attach the receipt, set paid/unpaid, and pick a category — that small bit of structure is what makes the charts, filters, and exports genuinely useful later.
</Callout>
