## How to use it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload a photo">
    Tap the upload button, pick a picture, and add a caption or tag (season, activity).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap to zoom">
    Click any photo to open it big. Swipe or use arrow keys to go to the next one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter">
    Show only summer photos, only fishing trips, and so on — use the filter buttons at the top.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Sign in required">
  You'll need an [account](/help/getting-started/accounts-and-login) to view or upload.
</Callout>
