Tasks is a shared to-do list for cabin chores and projects. Three columns: **To Do**, **Doing**, **Done**.

<LinkCard
  href="/todos"
  title="Open tasks"
  description="See the board"
/>

## How to use it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a task">
    Tap **Add Task**. Write a short title and any details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Move it along">
    When you start working on something, move it to **Doing**. When it's finished, move it to **Done**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drag to reorder">
    Drag cards up and down to prioritize, or drag between columns.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Sign in required">
  The board needs an [account](/help/getting-started/accounts-and-login).
</Callout>
